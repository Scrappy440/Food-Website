About Us

Founded in 2025, BiteThat! has been on a mission to provide users with detailed information about the food they consume, to better understand their dietary needs.

We believe that everyone deserves to have access to a user-friendly space where they can explore and learn about nutrition, recipes, and healthy eating habits.
